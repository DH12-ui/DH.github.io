飞影

简介

One of the Armor Warriors, appearing in the first episode. Originally a Yanxing vice-general-level armor, the Lufa army was brought back to Ares after breaking the Yanxing, and after transformation, it was developed in batches, and became a combat weapon used by Ares warriors to maintain security in the galaxy and defend justice in the galaxy. The Flying Shadow armor is mainly blue, and the speed, jump, and explosiveness are the strongest of the three armors, but because it is a light armor, it is at a loss in defense and endurance.
