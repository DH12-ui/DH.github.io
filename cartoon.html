虹猫蓝兔七侠传

In 397 AD, in the beautiful Zhangjiajie, the Demon Sect led by the Black Heart Tiger set fire to the mountain, poisoned the living beings, and wanted to catch the jade unicorn, the spirit beast of the forest, in a vain attempt to enhance internal strength and dominate the martial arts by drinking the blood of the unicorn. In order to maintain the peace and tranquility of the forest, Rainbow Cat's father joined forces with Blue Rabbit's mother and other seven people, seven swords combined, and finally defeated the Black Heart Tiger, but the seven swords were either injured or disabled. Fifty years later, the Black Heart Tiger led the Demon Sect to make a comeback. And the only thing that can stop the Black Heart Tiger is to merge the seven swords again, but at this time, except for the father of the rainbow cat, the other six swords have long been scattered and secluded, and their whereabouts are unknown. In a bloody battle, Rainbow Cat's father was finally outnumbered and died heroically. The young hero Red Cat obeyed his father's orders and shouldered the heavy responsibility of saving the peace of the forest, and went down the mountain in anger to find the other six swords...

铠甲勇士刑天

Xiao Tian, Xiao Fei, and Xiao Gang were originally just three ordinary young people with completely different personalities, ideals and family circumstances in "Hope City", because of their special talents, they lived under the same roof in the karmic meeting, welcomed and met the same mysterious cultivators, signed an armor contract, obtained the ability to summon high-energy combat armor originating from the planet Ares and combined with the highest technology crystallization of the universe, and became a new generation of armored warriors representing the power of justice - Torturer, Flying Shadow and King Kong, Wage a resolute struggle against the Nether Legion headed by Emperor Lufa and its minions. "Armor Warrior Punishment Day" is the second part of the "Armor Warrior" series, a special photography masterpiece with a Douban score of 9.2, which is the pinnacle of domestic live-action special photography. Produced by Guangdong Aofei Animation Co., Ltd., directed by Zhang Hanwen, written by Wu Chengqi, starring Li Wei, Wu Haoze, Zhang Yanan and others. The general armor originally belonged to Yanxing.
